Privacy Policy — Mogura

Effective date / 生效日期: June 10, 2026

English

Mogura ("the App") is a macOS application developed by Yuxin Gao ("we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

In short: Mogura processes everything locally on your Mac. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

1. Information we do not collect

Mogura has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We do not collect, store on our servers, or receive from you any personal information, media files, transcripts, or usage data.

2. On-device processing

All transcription and subtitle editing happen entirely on your Mac using on-device speech-recognition models (Whisper, running locally via Apple's Metal). Your audio and video files are processed locally and are never uploaded to us or to any third party for transcription.

3. Network access

The App accesses the network for a single purpose: to download the Whisper model file you choose, from a source you select — Hugging Face or ModelScope. When you start a download, your device connects directly to that provider to retrieve the file. We receive no information from this connection. Those providers may process the request (e.g. server logs including your IP address) under their own privacy policies:

  • Hugging Face — https://huggingface.co/privacy
  • ModelScope — https://www.modelscope.cn (see their site for their privacy terms)

The App makes no other network requests. It does not "phone home", check in, or send telemetry.

4. Where your data is stored

Everything the App creates stays on your Mac:

  • Projects and subtitles are stored in a local database inside the App's sandbox container.
  • Downloaded model files are stored locally on disk.
  • Exported subtitle files (SRT / VTT / ASS) are saved to the location you choose.

The App runs in the macOS App Sandbox and accesses only the files you explicitly select, using macOS security-scoped bookmarks.

5. Data sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any of your data — because we never collect it in the first place.

6. Children's privacy

Mogura is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Your control

Because your data lives only on your Mac, you are in full control. You can delete any project from within the App (which also revokes its saved file access), remove downloaded models in Settings → Models, or uninstall the App to remove all of its data.

8. Changes to this policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Effective date" above. Material changes will be reflected in an App update.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at hughadward123@gmail.com.

中文

Mogura（"本应用"）是由 Yuxin Gao（"我们"）开发的 macOS 应用。本隐私政策说明本应用如何处理你的信息。

一句话：Mogura 的所有处理都在你的 Mac 本地完成。我们不收集、不传输、也不出售任何个人数据。

1. 我们不收集的信息

本应用没有用户账号、没有数据统计、没有广告、没有任何形式的追踪。我们不会收集、不会在服务器上存储、也不会从你那里接收任何个人信息、媒体文件、字幕内容或使用数据。

2. 本地处理

所有转写和字幕编辑都完全在你的 Mac 上、使用本地的语音识别模型（Whisper，通过 Apple Metal 本地运行）完成。你的音视频文件在本地处理，绝不会为了转写而上传给我们或任何第三方。

3. 网络访问

本应用访问网络仅有一个用途：下载你选择的 Whisper 模型文件，来源由你选择——Hugging Face 或 ModelScope。当你发起下载时，你的设备会直接连接该提供方获取文件，我们不会从该连接中获得任何信息。这些提供方可能依据其各自的隐私政策处理该请求（例如包含你 IP 地址的服务器日志）：

  • Hugging Face — https://huggingface.co/privacy
  • ModelScope — https://www.modelscope.cn（其隐私条款请见其网站）

本应用不发起任何其它网络请求，不会"回传"、不会签到、不会发送遥测数据。

4. 数据存储位置

本应用产生的一切都保留在你的 Mac 上：

  • 项目和字幕存储在本应用沙箱容器内的本地数据库中。
  • 下载的模型文件存储在本地磁盘。
  • 导出的字幕文件（SRT / VTT / ASS）保存到你指定的位置。

本应用运行于 macOS App Sandbox，仅通过 macOS 安全作用域书签访问你明确选择的文件。

5. 数据共享

我们不共享、不出售、不出租、不披露你的任何数据——因为我们从一开始就不收集它。

6. 儿童隐私

Mogura 并非面向儿童，也不会在知情情况下收集任何人（包括 13 岁以下儿童）的信息。

7. 你的控制权

由于你的数据只存在于你的 Mac 上，你拥有完全的控制权。你可以在应用内删除任意项目（同时会撤销其已保存的文件访问权），在 设置 → Models 中删除已下载的模型，或卸载应用以移除其全部数据。

8. 本政策的变更

如本隐私政策有更新，我们将修改上方的"生效日期"。重大变更将通过应用更新体现。

9. 联系方式

对本隐私政策有任何疑问？请联系 hughadward123@gmail.com。
