Transcribe

On-device Whisper, accelerated by Metal.

Drop in a video or audio file and get accurate, timed subtitles automatically. Choose a Small model for speed or a Large one for the best accuracy — it all runs locally. And it transcribes 99 languages, detected automatically — from English and Spanish to Japanese, Arabic, and Hindi.

Edit

A transcript that follows along.

Review and fix subtitles in a clean editor that stays in sync with playback. Click any line to jump there; the active line highlights as the video plays. Keyboard controls keep editing fast and focused.

Export clean, standard subtitle files for YouTube, Premiere Pro, Final Cut, and most editors and players. What you see is exactly what ships out.
